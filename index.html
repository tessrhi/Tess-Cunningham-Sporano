    About Me

    My name is Tess Cunningham and I'm currently a student at Loyola University New Orleans studying Music Indsutry and minoring in classical voice. I've been taking vocal lessons and participating in choirs for 8 years. I've always had a love for classical music and have also played voilin in orchestras. I now take private vocal lessons with Dreux Montegut. I'm skillful in sight singing and can learn classical pieces quickly. My repertoire includes pieces in French, Italian, Latin, English, and German.
